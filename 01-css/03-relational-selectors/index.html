Relational Selectors

Select elements based on their relationship with other elements.

Achieve cleaner markup but can be fragile because they depend on their location in the DOM. Also, they are not as fast as basic selectors. So do not optimize your design early.

As Donald Knuth said, Premature optimization is the root of all evils.
